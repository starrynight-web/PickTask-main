Frequently Asked Questions

Find quick answers to common questions about PickTask

How do I get started with PickTask?

Getting started is easy! Simply sign up for an account, choose a plan that fits your needs, and start creating tasks and projects. You can invite team members, set deadlines, and customize your workflow in just a few clicks.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes to your plan will be reflected in your next billing cycle, and we'll prorate any differences in cost.

Is my data secure with PickTask?

Absolutely. We take security seriously. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest, and we regularly perform security audits. We're also compliant with GDPR and other major privacy regulations.

Do you offer team or enterprise plans?

Yes, we offer customized enterprise plans with additional features like advanced permissions, dedicated support, and enhanced security. Contact our sales team for more information.
