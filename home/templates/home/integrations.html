How to Set Up Google Calendar Integration

Follow these simple steps to connect your Google Calendar with PickTask

1

Go to your Integrations settings

Navigate to your account settings and select the "Integrations" tab.

2

Click on Google Calendar

Find the Google Calendar integration card and click the "Connect" button.

3

Authorize the connection

You'll be redirected to Google to authorize the connection between PickTask and your calendar.

4

Configure your preferences

Choose which calendars to sync and set your notification preferences.

5

Start using the integration

Your tasks and events will now sync automatically between PickTask and Google Calendar.
