Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our pricing and beta program

How long will the beta phase last?
We haven't set an exact end date for our beta phase. We want to ensure PickTask is fully polished and meets all user needs before we officially launch. We'll provide at least 60 days notice before any pricing changes take effect.
What happens when the beta ends?
When the beta phase ends, we'll introduce our official pricing plans. However, all beta users will receive special perks, which may include permanent discounts, extended free access, or premium features at no additional cost.
Will my data be safe during the beta?
Absolutely. We take data security seriously. All user data is encrypted and backed up regularly. When we transition from beta to our official launch, your data will be preserved and seamlessly migrated.
How can I provide feedback during the beta?
We welcome all feedback! You can use the feedback button within the app, join our beta community forum, or contact our support team directly. Your suggestions help us improve PickTask for everyone.
